About VOUCH

VOUCH solves the "Experience Paradox" for university students: you can't get a job without experience, and you can't get experience without a job. We offer simulated, real-world projects from actual companies, allowing students to build a verifiable "Proof-of-Work" portfolio that supersedes the traditional resume.

For Students

Build verifiable proof-of-work portfolios through real company projects. Break the experience paradox and enter the workforce with proven skills.

For Universities

Improve graduate employability rates and justify tuition ROI. Monitor student engagement and identify practical skill gaps.

For Corporations

Access pre-vetted talent in a skills-first economy. Reduce hiring time by 40% with candidates who have already proven their abilities.
